Job Title: Data Analyst

Company: NovaTech Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

Location: Bangalore, India (Hybrid)

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated Data Analyst to join our analytics team. The ideal candidate will have strong analytical skills, experience with data visualization tools, and the ability to transform raw data into actionable insights. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams including Product, Marketing, and Engineering to drive data-driven decision making.

Responsibilities:
- Collect, clean, and analyze structured and unstructured data from multiple sources.
- Develop dashboards and reports using Power BI/Tableau for business stakeholders.
- Conduct statistical analysis to identify trends, correlations, and business opportunities.
- Collaborate with engineers to improve data pipelines and ensure data accuracy.
- Communicate insights clearly through visualizations and presentations.

Requirements:
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field.
- 1–3 years of proven experience in data analysis or business intelligence.
- Proficiency in SQL, Python (Pandas, NumPy), and Excel.
- Experience with data visualization tools (Power BI, Tableau, or similar).
- Strong knowledge of statistical methods and hypothesis testing.
- Excellent communication and problem-solving skills.

Preferred:
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP).
- Familiarity with machine learning concepts.

Salary: Competitive package based on experience.
